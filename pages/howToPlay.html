How to Play

The goal of Berry Picker (tm) is to match the berry with its assigned basket. Refer to the rules below:

  1. Input your first and last name and press 'submit'
  2. Drag the berry into its matching basket
  3. If the berry is dropped into the correct basket, it will turn green
  4. A scoreboard will be provided to show how many you got correct
  5. To win, drop all the berries in the right basket!

To try again and reach a 4/4 on the scoreboard refresh the page!
