How to Play

The goal of Berry Picker tm is to match the berry with its assigned basket. Refer to the rules below:

  • 1. Drag the berry into its matching basket
  • 2. If the berry is dropped into the correct basket, it will turn green
  • 3. If the berry is dropped into the wrong basket it will snap back in place
  • 4. To win, drop all the berries in the right basket!
